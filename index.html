Love Letter From babygirl

Babyyyyyyy , my gandu with the cutest nose, you annoy me so much but you are also the only person who makes the world feel like home, and I miss that. I miss your stupid voice notes, your random rants about people I don’t even know, the way you say “shaaaraan” when you’re mad but your eyes still soften, the way you light up when you talk about the smallest things like a puppy or an elephant.

You are the chaos I chose Sanika, and I’ll choose you in every lifetime, in every fight, in every bad day and every beautiful day. I don’t think you will ever fully understand what you did to my life the day you decided to trust me with your heart, even when I know it was hard for you to trust love, to trust someone like me, to trust the universe that maybe we were meant to happen. You made me feel alive after years of feeling nothing, you gave my days meaning, and even when we fought, I slept thinking about you, and woke up wanting to be better for you. You are that rare story I’ll tell my kids about, but they’ll never truly get what you meant to me, because you are the one person who can make me want to fight for a lifetime just to see your priceless smile.

I don’t care if the world says it’s crazy or impossible, but you and I, we have a kind of madness that makes even bad days worth it, and I want to live that madness forever with you. I want to be the one holding your hand when you’re scared, the one making you laugh when you want to cry, the one who buys you your favourite food even when I’m angry, the one who fights with you and then makes up within minutes because being mad at you hurts more than anything else. Sanika, I love you in a way that’s messy and real and honest, a love that is stupidly patient, a love that is loud but also soft when you need it to be, a love that chooses you every single day. I want to be there to watch you become everything you dream of, to see you glow up and conquer the world, but I also want to be there to hold you when you’re tired and just need to be the small girl you are inside.

Baby, if there is anything I’m sure of, it’s that I don’t want anyone else, I don’t want an easy love, I don’t want a quiet love, I want our kind of love. The love where I still keep the shirt I wore when I hugged you last, the love where every moment is a memory I can’t let go of, the love where your happiness will always be more important to me than my pride, the love where even when you try to leave, I’ll find a way to remind you that you are home with me. So yes, I’ll manifest you every single day, because it’s you, Sanika. Always you. Forever you. Let’s not let the world or a moment of confusion steal away the kind of love people search for their whole lives. Let’s fight for us. Let’s laugh again, let’s argue again, let’s love again. Because baby, we are worth it, you are worth it, and this love, our love, is worth every bit of the madness it brings.

I love you. Muah.
